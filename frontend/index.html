Our Legacy

The Art of
Perfume Making

A legacy of over 50 years in the Indian perfume industry, Ruh Imperium was founded in Kannauj with one mission — to bring the purest, most authentic attars directly from the distillery to your skin.

No unnecessary middlemen. Straight from India's perfume capital 'Kannauj'. Every bottle carries the wisdom of generations, the patience of the ancient deg-bhapka distillation process.

What is Attar?

Traditional perfume oil, made without alcohol

Attar is a concentrated perfume oil traditionally distilled from flowers, woods, roots, spices, and earth notes into a natural oil base. A tiny swipe warms on the skin and reveals its character slowly.

Ruh Imperium pairs classic Kannauj notes like Mitti, Mogra, Gulab, Hina, Kewra, and Sandalwood with modern EDP-style blends for daily wear, gifting, and special occasions.
