Our Legacy

The Art of
Perfume Making

A legacy of over 100 years in the Indian perfume industry, Ruh Imperium was founded in Kannauj with one mission — to bring the purest, most authentic attars directly from the distillery to your skin.

No unnecessary middlemen. Straight from India's perfume capital 'Kannauj'. Every bottle carries the wisdom of generations, the patience of the ancient deg-bhapka distillation process.

From Nature to You

Our Process

✦
01
🌾

Grow

We source fresh flowers, roots, and botanicals from India's finest herbalists and trusted farmers.

02
🌺

Harvest

Once in full bloom, the finest selection is hand-collected and brought to our oil distillery in Kannauj.

03
